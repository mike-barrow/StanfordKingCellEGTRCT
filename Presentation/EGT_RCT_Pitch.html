What Is Ergothioneine?

  • Essential Amino Acid
  • GRAS — safe, no known toxicity
  • Antioxidant — but not like the others
  • Highly stable — thione form resists autoxidation
  • Dedicated active transporter (OCTN1)
  • Capable of crossing BBB

Discovered 1909

It took nearly a century to understand
why biology bothered to keep it.

Why Biology Dedicated a Transporter

Most molecules diffuse or degrade. EGT has its own active import system.

  • OCTN1 (SLC22A4) — selectively transports EGT with Km ~20 µM, 10–100× higher affinity than any other substrate
  • Conserved across mammals, birds, fish — evolution maintained this system
  • Expressed in erythrocytes, liver, kidney, intestine, brain endothelium

Crosses the BBB: OCTN1 on brain capillary endothelium transports EGT into CNS. Detectable in CSF.

The Evolutionary Question

"Why does the genome dedicate
a transporter to a single amino acid?"

Organisms that could scavenge and retain EGT
out-reproduced those that could not.

The Gap This RCT Fills

We have epidemiology — 21-year mortality data
We have mechanisms — SIRT6, MPST, Nrf2, telomeres
We have clinical pilots — cognition, sleep, ovarian reserve

What we don't have:

A benchmark against the gold standard of biological aging.

Corley 2025 proved metabolic interventions shift GrimAge2 (−2.3 yr), PhenoAge (−4.9 yr), DunedinPACE (−0.09) in 32 weeks. EGT's SIRT6 mechanism is chromatin-based — a different path to the same endpoint.

RCT Design Snapshot

ParameterDetail
DesignDouble-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel 1:1
N160 (80/arm) · 50–70 yr · healthy
DoseEGT 30 mg/day vs microcrystalline cellulose placebo
Duration12 months core + optional 18-month extension
PrimaryGrimAge2 EAA → PhenoAge → DunedinPACE (hierarchical)
Secondary mech.Plasma EGT, IL-6, hs-CRP, TNFα, 8-OHdG, GSH/GSSG, OCTN1 genotype
Secondary func.Cognition (Cogstate/NIH Toolbox), skin aging, grip strength, gait speed
ExploratoryPSQI sleep, FSS fatigue, optional multi-omics sub-study
SiteFifth Affiliated Hospital, Sun Yat-sen University (Zhuhai) + King Cell Jiamei
Power80% for δ = 0.50 in GrimAge2 EAA, α = 0.05 two-sided

All three clocks from a single Illumina EPIC v2.0 array — no additional sample required.
