## Summary

- **Observational studies** consistently link higher EGT levels (or mushroom intake as proxy) with lower mortality, better cognition, and better physical function
- **Pure EGT RCTs** are few (3 completed, 1 open-label) but uniformly show safety and encouraging signals across cognition, sleep, and metabolic domains
- **No study has tested EGT against epigenetic aging clocks** — this is the gap our RCT fills
