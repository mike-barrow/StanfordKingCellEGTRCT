## 1. OTHER COMPOUND: BUTYRATE (CONFIDENTIAL)
Snyder is running (or planning) a study with **butyrate** — a short-chain fatty acid (SCFA)
and HDAC inhibitor with anti-inflammatory and geroscience-adjacent properties. He wants to
add EGT as a comparative arm alongside butyrate vs shared placebo.

**Implication:** Three-arm design: EGT vs Butyrate vs Placebo. Shared placebo group.
Common endpoints, same infrastructure. Both compounds get independent publications.

**Dose signal:** He asked if EGT is water-soluble (yes) and expected **much higher doses**
than the 30mg proposed — mentioned **3g** (3000mg). Current human data only goes to
120mg/day (PMID 42310611, 2026, safe). EGT is GRAS and water-soluble so higher doses
may be feasible but have no human safety data above 120mg. This needs discussion.
